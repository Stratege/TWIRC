read the config.ini it contains all the startup information

commands are partially case sensitive (if they aren't for passing through) - this means /Select is okay but /SelECT isn't

Channels can be switched and opened (not joined!) via /select channelname or /s channelname - this also includes queries (and is, infact the main purpose)

Text can be scrolled with up/down arrow, page up/down and pos1/last buttons.
Channels can be scrolled via ctrl+up/down arrow

To close a channel, type /close channelname or /c channelname
To close every channel, type /closeall

To copy  the lowest visible line into the clipboard, type /copy
